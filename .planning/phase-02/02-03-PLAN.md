  <how-to-verify>
Build and install to device first:
```
xcodegen generate && xcodebuild build -project Surfvid.xcodeproj -scheme Surfvid \
  -destination 'generic/platform=iOS' CODE_SIGNING_REQUIRED=NO CODE_SIGNING_ALLOWED=NO
```
Then open in Xcode and run on the physical device. Select a large video from the library and exercise each item:

**A. Scrub feel (SKIM-01, D-04)**
1. Tap a video in the library to open skim screen
2. Drag slowly left and right on the video surface
3. Expected: frame updates directionally — drag right = earlier in video, drag left = later
4. Drag fast left and right
5. Expected: fast drag covers more of the video; feels proportional (not locked to finger position)
6. Expected: playback was paused automatically on first drag (D-03)

**B. Timecode (SKIM-06)**
7. Observe the timecode label (top-left of bottom chrome) while dragging
8. Expected: timecode updates continuously — no freezing, no jumping backward
9. Tap play, let video run for 5 seconds
10. Expected: timecode updates every ~0.1s during playback

**C. Chrome toggle (D-01)**
11. Tap once on the video surface (not on any button)
12. Expected: chrome (buttons, timecode, timeline) fades out in ~0.2s
13. Tap again
14. Expected: chrome fades back in

**D. Play/Pause (SKIM-04, D-02)**
15. Tap the play button (▶ icon) in the bottom chrome
16. Expected: video plays, button changes to ⏸
17. Tap pause (⏸)
18. Expected: video pauses, button changes to ▶

**E. In/Out marking (SKIM-03, SKIM-07, SKIM-08)**
19. Scrub to a position in the video
20. Tap IN button
21. Expected: accent-colored flash band appears at top-left edge for ~700ms
22. Expected: "MARKING · IN @ M:SS.f" pill appears below top chrome
23. Expected: pending-In marker line appears in the timeline bar
24. Scrub forward a few seconds
25. Tap OUT button
26. Expected: white flash band appears at top-right edge for ~700ms
27. Expected: pending-In pill disappears
28. Expected: colored clip range appears in the timeline bar
29. Expected: clip count label increments to "1 marked"

**F. Multiple clips (SKIM-08)**
30. Repeat steps 19-29 two more times at different positions
31. Expected: "3 marked" in the bottom chrome
32. Expected: three colored clip ranges visible in the timeline bar

**G. Out-before-In (D-07)**
33. Without tapping IN, tap OUT at some position
34. Expected: a clip is registered with autoIn = current_time - 15s (visible in timeline)

**H. Performance — no stutter on large file (PERF-02, SKIM-01)**
35. Select the largest video available (ideally 30+ minutes or multi-GB)
36. Scrub aggressively for 30 seconds
37. Expected: UI remains responsive; timecode updates; no memory warnings; no crashes

Record any issues found.
